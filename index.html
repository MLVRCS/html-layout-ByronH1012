The history of cars

Cars were invented in 1886, when German inventor Carl Benz patented his Benz Patent-Motorwagen. Cars became widely available during the 20th century. One of the first cars affordable by the masses was the 1908 Model T, an American car manufactured by the Ford Motor Company.

Stance culture originated in Japan by drifters and outlaws groups like Japanese Bosozuku. For drifters, the lower they could get their cars to the track the more grip and handling they could get. But for groups like the Bosozuku, lowering a car made them stand out from the rest of the domestic vehicles on the road.
